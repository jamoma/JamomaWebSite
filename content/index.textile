<p>Jamoma is a <a href="http://jamoma.org/forum.html">community</a>-based project, dedicated to the development of several toolsets and frameworks for artistic creation through digital means. 
The project is led by an international <a href="http://jamoma.org/team.html">team</a> of academic researchers, artists and developers, and supported by several <a href="http://jamoma.org/support.html">institutions</a></p>
