<h2 class="lead">Jamoma’s Essence is Modularity</h2>

<p class="lead"> Manage complex systems built up of flexible and interchangable modules. <br/> Make your own modules to fully leverage Jamoma’s power or use the existing library to quickly create and explore artistic potentials.</p>

<p class="lead"> Explore Jamoma’s powerful communication, automation, and signal processing in a graphical patching environment like Max or take it close to the metal and use the C++ systems directly. </p>
