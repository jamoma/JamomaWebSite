p(small). _Q: How do you see if a patch is made with Jamoma 0.5.7 or Jamoma 1?_
A: The names of externals in Jamoma 0.5.7 started with a <code>jcom.</code> suffix. The names of modules would have a <code>jmod.</code> prefix. In Jamoma&nbsp;v.1 externals have a <code>j.</code> prefix, and the names of model and view patchers ends with <code>model.maxpat</code> and <code>view.maxpat</code>.
